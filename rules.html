Rules

Clan Guidelines & Expectations

Basic Guidelines

Following Orders : You are to follow all orders given by the seniors unless or until they are directed against the community itself or the monarchs of the community.

Stay Respectful : Here in Dnmx, you are expected to stay respectful in all times. We're all comrades/mates here and its best if we stay united. Respecting other player's beliefs & boundaries is essential for guaranteeing unity.

Stay Loyal : Loyalty is the utmost requirement of staying in Dnmx. Sometimes people may not have as much time to give to Dnmx as before, but even if you've given years to Dnmx and aren't loyal to it, that won't be a positive factor at all. Sharing information of ANY kind (for example finances, numbers, resources, appointments) to any other player outside Dnmx may get you in severe trouble within the community. Even attempting to spy within our community or betraying our community may get you in trouble and do not even think you would be safe. We may have eyes and ears where you may not even think.

Language Usage : While in Dnmx, please make sure to not use any kind of cuss/slur words and make sure to address the seniors with their respective titles, unless they give you the freehand to not do so, even if they do, make sure to address them in a formal way in public or when in front of non-Dnmx people. This would help in keeping our professional image safeguarded in front of other players.

Do NOT Break ANY MineMalia Rules : Beware! If you're found breaking the MineMalia rules, we may not be merciful on you at all. You are completely allowed to report ANY senior ranking officer of Dnmx to the MineMalia Staff Team in case you believe they're breaking the MineMalia rules. Even if they get banned from MineMalia, you may not be kicked from Dnmx. That's the level of justice we believe in.

Internal Conflicts : Make sure to not start any type of internal conflicts as this acts as a barrier in the general unity of the community. You may face severe punishment if you attempt to start fights or engage in ongoing fights in the clan. You are to report all growing or unnoticed conflicts to your seniors.

Favoring Members : We're all mates here. If you think any of your mates in Dnmx needs any kind of assistance, try your best to fulfil their needs. We're all like one body here. If one lacks behind, it may make all others lack behind too. We must act as a single unit.

Nickname : It is mandatory for ranked people to put Dnmx in their /nickname. Be patriotic.

Other Important Guidelines

Monarch Contact : Do not ping or try to contact the respected Monarch(s). You should first contact your seniors and if they think your matter is of a serious intensity, they may approach the executive council members. If the executive council members (excl. monarch) believe the matter is of serious intensity and worthy of being discussed with the monarch(s), they will do so.

Bans : Anyone not following the above mentioned guidelines or not abiding by common sense will be dealt with. There can sometimes be room for chances, but not for compromises. We always reserve the right to ban/kick you from the community.

Follow the above mentioned rules and let's together build this a community we'll be proud of!

May we succeed in achieving dominance and success.
